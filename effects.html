Firing of teachers due to budget cuts

Students end up wandering around after school, and are more likely to get ito trouble. Lack of extracurricular activities makes weak candidates in college admissions. After school activities and sports allow for students to try new things and become well rouned students.

Lack of class electives due to budget cuts
